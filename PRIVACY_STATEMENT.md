# Privacy Statement

Haven is designed to keep your information on your device.

## What data the app stores

- Core values you enter during setup
- Mission items and weekly selections
- Weekly intentions and reflections
- Evidence you add during a cycle
- Local app preferences such as tray and startup settings

## How data is handled

- Data is saved locally in the app's user data folder.
- The app does not require an account.
- The app does not sync data to a cloud service.
- The app does not send personal data to external services during normal use.
- The app does not use tracking or analytics scripts.

## Your controls

- You can export your data from Settings.
- You can remove all local data from Settings.
- You can change startup and tray preferences inside the app.

## Changes

If the app changes in the future, this statement should be updated to match the current behavior.
